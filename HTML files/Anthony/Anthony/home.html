⟶ Introduction about Covid-19 and the Pandemic ⟵



The Covid-19 pandemic , also known as the coronavirus pandemic, was first indentified from an outbreak in China from December, 2019. The virus was unable to be contained, which then allowed it to spread worldwide, causing an outbreak. January 30, 2020 was the date that the World Health Organization declared a pandemic upon society. As of Febuary 2022, the pandemic has caused more than 406 million cases, and 5.79 million deaths, making it one of the most deadliest pandemics in history. The main question everyone is wondering is how long will this last and when will it be the end of this pandemic?
